The Foundations

This section covers what Organizational Psychology is and how psychologists study it. We'll explore the field's historical origins with the pivotal Hawthorne Studies and dive into the core research methods that generate specialized, evidence-based knowledge.

The Employee Journey

Here, we follow the employee's lifecycle from first contact to full integration. We'll cover how people are attracted to organizations, the critical stages of socialization to become an "insider," and the development of key attitudes like job satisfaction and organizational commitment.

Workplace Behaviors

This section examines the spectrum of employee actions and their impact on the organization. We'll differentiate between positive, productive behaviors like OCBs, and harmful, counterproductive behaviors (CWBs), exploring their causes and how they can be managed.
